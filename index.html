Well-seasoned design. I’m Dan, an experienced designer of the UX/UI flavour.

Over the years, I’ve been a user interface designer, a user experience designer, an information architect, a project manager and an occasional front-end dev.

I also ran two successful digital agencies in and around Bath, UK - Karyx and NSource.
